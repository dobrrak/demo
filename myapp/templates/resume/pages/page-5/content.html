The very first tweak

CD drive laser

in 2002

at the age of 8

    Then one day one cousin came up with a fix!
  1. First, disassemble the CD drive casing.
  2. Then plug the drive into PC naked.
  3. Put the CD in, boot Wins and try to open contents of this scratched cd. As expected, it did not work.
  4. Here comes the magic. See, there is a little screw near the laser. It is a set screw, which adjusts the laser lenses.
  5. Eject the CD. Use a screw driver to turn the screw by a quarter.
  6. Insert the CD again. Still not reading? Make another quarter turn.

  • After several turns it suddenly worked! I could read the CD contents and launch my game! Awesome!
  • As a final approach I created an ISO image with CloneCD. Sooner or later the CD will break completely. Better to emulate cdrom and mount ISO image. Also it is faster.

    • The worms in arms went to war again!
